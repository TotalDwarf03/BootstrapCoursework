Hi!

I'm Kieran, an apprentice software developer.

I've always enjoyed the technical aspects of everything - how things work and how they can be improved. Since I was young, I enjoyed creating "things". I used to spend hours building Lego and turning sets into my own creations. It was no supprise that I'd end up going down one of the engineering branches.

As I got older, I began to enjoy the digital world more and more. I enjoyed playing videogames as most people do nowadays, having a Nintendo Wii and eventually an Xbox 360. I began to spend more time being online so it was obvious that I'd have a fondness for ICT in school. It was through school that I gained an interest in a block programming language called Scratch. Me and my friends would spend ages creating, sharing and browsing Scratch projects which I think really gave me the bug for software development.

Since then, I've become really invested in becoming a developer. I've enjoyed seeing technology grow and hope to be apart of and on the forefront of that growth in the future.
